ROB MAN

John tells the man to give him everything in his pockets but the man does not oblige, the man further
humiliates John. John balls his fist and attemps to strike the man. John did'nt know that the man was
carrying a knife and was gripping it in his coat pocket during this exchange. Before Johns strike landed
he saw the gleam of the blade. It was at this moment John realized that he had screwed up. Johns strike never
landed. John was stabbed and bled out and died at Chimney Rock that night.
