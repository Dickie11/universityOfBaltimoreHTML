COMING HOME

in the year 1851 word had finally reached John Smith about James W. Marshall's discovery of gold
in Sutters Mill, California. People on the west coast were claiming to get rich from the precious metal.
After much consideration and debate, John Smith left his family in Missouri to become a 49'er and to persue
the riches men were speaking of found in California.It has been 14 months since he left home. He has had some
luck and has aquired a modest amount of gold, enought to pay for the equipment he had to loan to mine the gold.
