SCOTT'S BLUFF

John arrives at Scott's Bluff. He encounters a group of men looking to join the 49'ers traveling to California
to try to cash in on the riches reported from the gold rush. John tells the men of his success in the short time
he was there mining and sifting. The men are intrigued and offer him food and alcohol. They talk and drink all night
John falls asleep next to the fire. When he wakes he realizes that all of his money has been stolen. He can
not continue for much longer without any money. John packs his belongings up and continues to Chimney Rock.
